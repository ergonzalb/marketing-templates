# Vendor Research Checklist
## 25-Point System for Vetting WarriorPlus Vendors

### How to Use This Checklist
- Score each item: ✅ (Pass), ⚠️ (Caution), ❌ (Fail)
- Any ❌ should be a red flag
- Multiple ⚠️ should make you think twice
- Use alongside Vendor Verdict for maximum protection

---

## SECTION 1: VENDOR REPUTATION (Points 1-7)

### 1. Vendor History & Track Record
- [ ] How long has the vendor been on WarriorPlus?
  - ✅ 2+ years
  - ⚠️ 6 months - 2 years
  - ❌ Less than 6 months

- [ ] How many products has the vendor launched?
  - ✅ 5+ products
  - ⚠️ 2-4 products
  - ❌ First product

- [ ] What's their overall vendor rating?
  - ✅ 4.5+ stars
  - ⚠️ 3.5-4.4 stars
  - ❌ Below 3.5 stars

### 2. Refund Rate Analysis
- [ ] What's the vendor's typical refund rate?
  - ✅ Under 10%
  - ⚠️ 10-20%
  - ❌ Over 20%

- [ ] Has the refund rate been increasing over time?
  - ✅ Stable or decreasing
  - ⚠️ Slight increase
  - ❌ Significant increase

### 3. Customer Satisfaction
- [ ] What do the reviews say?
  - ✅ Mostly positive (80%+)
  - ⚠️ Mixed (60-80%)
  - ❌ Mostly negative (under 60%)

- [ ] Are there common complaints?
  - ✅ No pattern of complaints
  - ⚠️ Minor issues mentioned
  - ❌ Same complaints repeatedly

### 4. Vendor Communication
- [ ] Does the vendor respond to support tickets?
  - ✅ Within 24 hours
  - ⚠️ Within 48 hours
  - ❌ Slow or no response

- [ ] Is the vendor active in the community?
  - ✅ Regularly visible
  - ⚠️ Occasionally active
  - ❌ Ghost vendor

### 5. Product Quality Consistency
- [ ] Do their products maintain similar quality?
  - ✅ Consistent quality
  - ⚠️ Some variation
  - ❌ Declining quality

- [ ] Are updates provided for products?
  - ✅ Regular updates
  - ⚠️ Occasional updates
  - ❌ Abandoned products

### 6. Affiliate Payment History
- [ ] Do they pay affiliates on time?
  - ✅ Always on time
  - ⚠️ Sometimes late
  - ❌ Payment issues reported

- [ ] Any complaints about commission clawbacks?
  - ✅ No issues
  - ⚠️ Minor issues
  - ❌ Frequent clawbacks

### 7. JV Page Professionalism
- [ ] Is the JV page professional and complete?
  - ✅ Complete with all details
  - ⚠️ Mostly complete
  - ❌ Missing key information

---

## SECTION 2: PRODUCT ANALYSIS (Points 8-14)

### 8. Product Relevance
- [ ] Does the product solve a real problem?
  - ✅ Clear problem/solution
  - ⚠️ Vague benefit
  - ❌ No clear value

- [ ] Is it relevant to your audience?
  - ✅ Perfect fit
  - ⚠️ Somewhat relevant
  - ❌ Poor fit

### 9. Sales Page Quality
- [ ] Is the sales page professional?
  - ✅ High quality
  - ⚠️ Average quality
  - ❌ Poor quality

- [ ] Are claims realistic?
  - ✅ Realistic promises
  - ⚠️ Slightly hyped
  - ❌ Unrealistic claims

### 10. Product Uniqueness
- [ ] What makes this product different?
  - ✅ Clear unique angle
  - ⚠️ Minor differences
  - ❌ Same as competitors

- [ ] Is it a rehash of old products?
  - ✅ Fresh content
  - ⚠️ Some recycled content
  - ❌ Complete rehash

### 11. Funnel Structure
- [ ] Is the funnel reasonable?
  - ✅ Logical progression
  - ⚠️ Aggressive but fair
  - ❌ Excessive upsells

- [ ] Do the upsells provide real value?
  - ✅ Genuine additional value
  - ⚠️ Some value
  - ❌ Just extracting money

### 12. Product Delivery
- [ ] How is the product delivered?
  - ✅ Instant, professional delivery
  - ⚠️ Some delays
  - ❌ Poor delivery system

- [ ] Are there technical issues?
  - ✅ No reported issues
  - ⚠️ Minor issues
  - ❌ Major technical problems

### 13. Training/Support Quality
- [ ] Is training provided?
  - ✅ Comprehensive training
  - ⚠️ Basic training
  - ❌ No training

- [ ] Is support responsive?
  - ✅ Fast, helpful support
  - ⚠️ Slow but helpful
  - ❌ Poor support

### 14. Product Longevity
- [ ] Will this product be relevant in 6 months?
  - ✅ Evergreen or timely
  - ⚠️ Short-term relevance
  - ❌ Already outdated

---

## SECTION 3: MARKET DATA (Points 15-19)

### 15. Conversion Metrics
- [ ] What's the conversion rate?
  - ✅ Over 10%
  - ⚠️ 5-10%
  - ❌ Under 5%

- [ ] Is the EPC (Earnings Per Click) disclosed?
  - ✅ Transparent EPC data
  - ⚠️ Vague EPC claims
  - ❌ No EPC information

### 16. Sales Volume
- [ ] How many units have been sold?
  - ✅ Strong sales history
  - ⚠️ Moderate sales
  - ❌ Poor sales

- [ ] Is momentum building or declining?
  - ✅ Increasing momentum
  - ⚠️ Stable
  - ❌ Declining

### 17. Affiliate Competition
- [ ] How competitive is the launch?
  - ✅ Moderate competition
  - ⚠️ High competition
  - ❌ Oversaturated

- [ ] Are top affiliates promoting it?
  - ✅ Yes, respected affiliates
  - ⚠️ Some good affiliates
  - ❌ No quality affiliates

### 18. Commission Structure
- [ ] Is the commission rate fair?
  - ✅ 50%+ commission
  - ⚠️ 30-50% commission
  - ❌ Under 30% commission

- [ ] Are there commission bumps for performance?
  - ✅ Yes, incentive structure
  - ⚠️ Minimal incentives
  - ❌ No incentives

### 19. Cookie Duration
- [ ] How long is the tracking cookie?
  - ✅ 30+ days
  - ⚠️ 7-30 days
  - ❌ Under 7 days

---

## SECTION 4: RED FLAGS (Points 20-25)

### 20. Scam Indicators
- [ ] Does it promise "get rich quick"?
  - ✅ Realistic timeline
  - ⚠️ Optimistic claims
  - ❌ Unrealistic promises

- [ ] Are income screenshots verified?
  - ✅ Real, verified results
  - ⚠️ Unverified claims
  - ❌ Fake screenshots

### 21. Product Accessibility
- [ ] Is the product actually accessible after purchase?
  - ✅ Immediate access
  - ⚠️ Some delays
  - ❌ Access issues reported

- [ ] Are there hidden fees?
  - ✅ Transparent pricing
  - ⚠️ Some upsells required
  - ❌ Hidden costs

### 22. Vendor Transparency
- [ ] Is the vendor's identity clear?
  - ✅ Real person, clear identity
  - ⚠️ Some information
  - ❌ Anonymous or fake identity

- [ ] Can you find them outside WarriorPlus?
  - ✅ Active on social media/website
  - ⚠️ Limited presence
  - ❌ No external presence

### 23. Product Demo/Preview
- [ ] Is there a demo or preview available?
  - ✅ Yes, quality demo
  - ⚠️ Limited preview
  - ❌ No preview available

### 24. Affiliate Tools Provided
- [ ] Are promotional materials provided?
  - ✅ Complete tool kit
  - ⚠️ Basic materials
  - ❌ No affiliate support

### 25. Launch Timing
- [ ] Is this a good time to promote?
  - ✅ Strong launch window
  - ⚠️ Okay timing
  - ❌ Poor timing (holidays, conflicts)

## SCORING SYSTEM

### Calculate Your Score:
- Count ✅ = ___ x 4 = ___
- Count ⚠️ = ___ x 2 = ___
- Count ❌ = ___ x 0 = ___

**Total Score: ___ out of 100**

### Score Interpretation:
- **90-100:** Excellent vendor - Promote with confidence
- **70-89:** Good vendor - Proceed with standard caution
- **50-69:** Average vendor - Consider carefully
- **30-49:** Poor vendor - High risk, probably avoid
- **0-29:** Dangerous vendor - Do not promote

## QUICK REFERENCE: DEAL BREAKERS

**Never promote if you see:**
- ❌ Refund rate over 30%
- ❌ Fake income claims
- ❌ No product delivery
- ❌ Anonymous vendor with no track record
- ❌ Multiple reports of non-payment to affiliates
- ❌ Product doesn't match sales page description
- ❌ No customer support whatsoever

**Use Vendor Verdict alongside this checklist for best results!**

## ACTION STEPS

1. **Before promoting any vendor:**
   - Run through this checklist
   - Check Vendor Verdict grade
   - Research outside WarriorPlus

2. **Keep records:**
   - Save screenshots of stats
   - Document your research
   - Track your results

3. **Trust your gut:**
   - If something feels off, investigate
   - Don't let FOMO override caution
   - Your reputation is worth more than one commission

4. **Build relationships:**
   - Connect with vendors before promoting
   - Join affiliate groups for intel
   - Share information with trusted affiliates
