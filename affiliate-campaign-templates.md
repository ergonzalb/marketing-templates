## USAGE NOTES

### How to Use These Templates:

1. **Replace placeholders:**
   - [First Name] → Subscriber's first name
   - [YOUR LINK] → Your affiliate link
   - [Product Name] → Actual product name
   - [Date/Timeframe] → Specific dates

2. **Customize the voice:**
   - Add your personal stories
   - Adjust tone to match your brand
   - Include your own results/testimonials

3. **Schedule sequence:**
   - Email 1: 2-3 days before launch
   - Email 2: 1 day before launch
   - Email 3: Launch day morning
   - Email 4: Launch day evening
   - Email 5: Day 2 (FAQ/objections)
   - Email 6: Day 4 (follow-up to non-buyers)

4. **Track results:**
   - Open rates
   - Click-through rates
   - Conversion rates
   - Unsubscribe rates

### Subject Line Tips:
- Use brackets [LIVE NOW], [URGENT], [LAST CHANCE]
- Include specific numbers ($27, $342, 30-day)
- Ask questions
- Create curiosity gaps
- Use power words: "exclusive," "free," "save," "protect"

### Call-to-Action Tips:
- One clear CTA per email
- Use buttons or bold text
- Repeat CTA 2-3 times in longer emails
- Create urgency (time limits, scarcity)
