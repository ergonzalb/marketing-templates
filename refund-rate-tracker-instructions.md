## Key Metrics to Watch

### 🟢 Good Signs:
- Refund rate under 10%
- Consistent low refunds from same vendor
- Net profit growing over time

### 🟡 Warning Signs:
- Refund rate 10-20%
- Increasing refund trends
- Vendor with mixed results

### 🔴 Red Flags:
- Refund rate over 20%
- Sudden spike in refunds
- Multiple complaints about same vendor

```
=IF(B2="","",C2/B2*100)

```
=B2*E2

```

```
